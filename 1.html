Types of Headings

Heading 1

This is a paragraph under heading 1. It typically represents the main heading of the page or the most important section.

Heading 2

This is a paragraph under heading 2. It usually represents a major section within the content.

Heading 3

This is a paragraph under heading 3. It represents a subsection under heading 2.

Heading 4

This is a paragraph under heading 4. It represents a smaller subsection under heading 3.

Heading 5

This is a paragraph under heading 5. It is used for even smaller subsections under heading 4.

Heading 6

This is a paragraph under heading 6. It is the smallest and least significant heading, often used for minor details.
