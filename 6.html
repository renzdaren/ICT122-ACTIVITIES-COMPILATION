Non - Breaking Space(s)

Nonbreaking Spaces Example

An example of this technique appears in the movie "12 Angry Men."

Preventing Line Breaks in Names

Here is an example: "Martin Luther King Jr."

This ensures that the name stays together on one line.

Preventing Line Breaks in Dates

Here is an example: "April 8, 2025"

This ensures that the date stays together on one line.

Preventing Line Breaks in Phrases

Here is an example: "State of the Union Address"

This ensures that the phrase stays together on one line.

Preventing Line Breaks in Measurements

Here is an example: "10 kg of rice"

This ensures that the measurement stays together on one line.
